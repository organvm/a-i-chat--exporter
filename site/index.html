Export your ChatGPT conversations with zero cloud dependency.

Feature list

Built for one-off saves and serious archives.

The free tier keeps everyday exports simple. Pro unlocks API-powered bulk workflows for users exporting large conversation sets.

  • Single chat exports

    Export the active thread in 5 formats.

    The userscript injects a small menu into the ChatGPT sidebar and exports the currently open conversation without leaving the browser.

    • Markdown with optional front matter and metadata
    • Self-contained HTML with dark/light-aware styling
    • JSON, PNG screenshot, and plain-text output
  • Local by design

    No cloud, no account lock-in.

    Exports are generated in-browser with your existing session credentials and downloaded to your device.

    • Works with Tampermonkey / Violentmonkey
    • No uploads of chat content by default
    • Filename templates for better filing and retrieval
  • Pro workflows

    Batch and conversion tooling when you need scale.

    Verified Pro users can process multiple conversations from ChatGPT API-backed sources and convert conversation JSON for downstream tools.

    • Bulk export from full conversation history
    • Project-scoped filtering and multi-select
    • TavernAI and Ooba conversion options

Export formats

Portable files, not another silo.

Every export is produced in your browser from your existing session. Files download locally and stay under your control.

  • .mdReadable Markdown with front matter and LaTeX preservation.
  • .htmlSelf-contained pages with light and dark styling support.
  • .jsonRaw conversation data for analysis, pipelines, and backups.
  • .pngFull-thread screenshots for sharing visual records.
  • .txtClean plain text copied directly to the clipboard.
